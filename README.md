# Privacy Policy — Pet the Dog (But Not Too Much)

**Effective date:** 2026/Jun/06
**App package:** `com.chronovision.petthedog`
**Developer:** Chronovision

---

## TL;DR

The game is a casual single-player Android app. We don't ask you to sign up, we don't collect your name or email, and we don't sell anything. The only data leaving your phone is what's needed to (1) show ads, (2) put your high score on a leaderboard if you choose to use it.

---

## 1. Data we collect ourselves

**None.** We do not run a server. We do not collect your name, email, location, or photos. All of your gameplay stats (Perfect / Sad / Angry / Treats counters, mute state, session count, the away-meadow end time) live exclusively in `SharedPreferences` on your device. Uninstalling the app erases all of it.

## 2. Data third-party SDKs collect

The app uses three Google services. Each is governed by Google's privacy policy in addition to this one.

### 2.1 Google AdMob (advertising)

We use Google AdMob to show interstitial and rewarded ads.

* **What it collects:** advertising ID, approximate IP address, device type, language, ad interaction events.
* **Why:** to serve and measure ads.
* **Your control:** Android Settings → Google → Ads lets you reset your advertising ID and opt out of personalised ads.
* **Policy:** <https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads>

### 2.2 Google User Messaging Platform (GDPR / IAB TCF consent)

If you are in the EU / UK / Switzerland, the app shows a consent dialog the first time it launches. Your consent choices are stored on the device and used by AdMob to decide whether ads can be personalised.

* **Policy:** <https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies>

### 2.3 Google Play Games Services (leaderboards)

Tapping the **Top** button asks you to sign in to Google Play Games. If you sign in, Google associates your Google Play Games player ID with your high score for the "Perfect rounds" leaderboard.

* **What it collects:** Google Play Games player ID and your submitted score.
* **Why:** to show Daily / Weekly / All-Time rankings.
* **Your control:** you can refuse sign-in (the rest of the game still works), reset your Play Games profile in the Play Games app, or delete your data per Google's instructions.
* **Policy:** <https://policies.google.com/privacy>

## 3. Permissions the app requests

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `INTERNET` | Required by AdMob and Play Games Services |
| `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` | AdMob uses it to avoid loading ads when offline |
| `com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID` | Lets AdMob read the advertising ID for ad targeting (Android 13+) |

The app does **not** ask for location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage.

## 4. Children

The app is **not directed to children under 13**. Per Google Play's Families policy, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we'll remove anything we can.

## 5. Data retention

* Local gameplay data: kept until you uninstall the app or clear its data in Android Settings.
* AdMob and Play Games data: retained per Google's policies.

## 6. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA)

You have the right to:

* Know what data we and our third parties collect (see sections 1–2).
* Request deletion of any third-party data — see the relevant Google policy links above. Local-only data is deleted by uninstalling.
* Opt out of personalised advertising at any time via Android Settings → Google → Ads.

## 7. Changes to this policy

We'll update this page if anything changes. The "Effective date" at the top reflects the most recent update.
